FAQs

  • What is the Builder Basics Bootcamp?

    This is a 6-week cohort-style enablement program designed to provide new builders with foundational skills to ensure they have a basic competency with WalkMe. Each week, you will learn a new builder skill set through a mix of self-paced learning, a weekly live session with an instructor, and exercises.

  • How much time should learners dedicate to the program weekly?

    A minimum of 3 hours will be required each week, but we recommend 3-6 hours. The more time dedicated to learning and building now, the more efficient and effective building will be long term.

  • What is the difference between the Bootcamp and the content available in the Digital Adoption Institute?

    The Digital Adoption Institute provides self-paced content that anyone can take at any point in their WalkMe journey. The purpose of the Bootcamp is to help new users get started, by providing them with a package of DAI self-serve content to complete each week, paired with exercises to practice their knowledge and a live Learning Lab to ask questions and get feedback on their exercises.
